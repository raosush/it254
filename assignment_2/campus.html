Campus Life

Campus life at NITK is one of the most enriching experiences a person could have in his/her life. The joy of staying at NITK hostels & being able to hangout with your batchmates/classmates etc is extremely joyful. NITK campus spreads across a vast area of approximately 400 acres. NITK campus is spread on either side of NH 66. NITK is one of the rare colleges which boasts of having a dedicated beach for itself.

Amenities

  • Private Beach
  • Guest House
  • Sports Complex with support for all kinds of sports
  • Central Computing Center
  • Health Care Center
  • Open Air Theatre
  • Silver Jublie Auditorium(SJA)
  • Hostels
  • Canteens
