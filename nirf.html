NIRF Ranking of NITK

NITK has contantly been rising in the NIRF rankings. In this year, NITK has been placed at 13th position among all other engineering colleges in the NIRF rankings. NITK secured a really good score of 61.30 in the NIRF ranking this year. NITK was placed at the 21st position in the previous year.
